For the finished lab file, you use your mouse to draw on the canvas.

Press and hold the left mouse button to draw.

Press the "F" key to switch between draw mode and eraser mode.

Press the "R" key to reset the canvas to its original state.


For the unfinished lab file, the controls are different. The instructions are in the comments at the top of the file.
